Our Story

Back in the late 90's Ramesh Jain, the current Managing Director had a small portable unit through which he used to sell shirts at various locations. It was only a matter of time before his hard work paid off. Together with his brother, Kishore Jain, the present Founder Chairman, they got together to create a modern and versatile brand.
The name Passion was chosen as this was a calling that both of them are thoroughly passionate about. Not only this, each product that comes out from the factory is a product of absolutely Passion for the profession, perseverance and innovation.

Why us!

Ethical Manufacturers

We ensure that all the garments are made as per ethical and fair trade standards. We empower women, advocate for flexible working hours, have appropriate wage systems and no child labour.

We do everything

We are renowned for being the one stop shop for a complete manufacturing process. Just provide us with the tech packs and we will take care of the rest. All your manufacturing will be catered to and completed by us.

Fashion Experts

We have our in house fashion designers with vast experience who can blow your mind with the designs they create. All while following the latest trends.

Experience

With nearly 30 years of experience in the garment industry, we at Passion Apparels culminate classical and tested techniques and designs as well as modern and futuristic aspects. We pride ourselves on being at the forefront of development of or domain due to our vast experience.

Skilled Workforce

The strong and skilled workforce forms the foundation of any organisation. Our workforce undergo continuous training and development to ensure that high standards and quality is met.

Garment Finishing

A wide range of protective as well as stylish finishes are provided based on requirements of our customers. We have an eye detail, making sure even the smallest things are taken care of, all taking place in a controlled environment.
