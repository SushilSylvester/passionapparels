How we do it differently

The process of Manufacturing is one of the most important aspects in ensuring that our products are of the best quality. We work tirelessly to improve our technology continuously and also ensure that our process is close to perfection.

Your ideas - design and tech packs

It all starts with providing a blueprint of your clothing idea.

Pattern and Sample making

Post the confirmation of the tech pack, we develop a prototype of the clothing design which finalses the various aspects of it.

Sample Approval and Pattern Grading

The developed sample is then approved by you. In the very rare cases that it’s not, a new prototype is developed.

Approval for mass production

We finally come to the production of your apparel. An approximate lead time of 45 days is given, but this depends on the complexity of the order.

Dispatch, Logistics and Reorder

The apparel is then sent with a waterproof packaging. We also have the facility for door to door delivery.
